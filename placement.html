Placement

Dear Recruiters,

We are immensely happy to launch a placement brochure for the current Industrial/Organizational batch of Department of Psychology, SNDT Women’s University, Churchgate. We have made this a studentdriven project and presented department activities as well as the learning experience provided at our University. As you flip through the pages, we wish to provide you a glimpse into the world of I/O Psychology and its application in organizational effectiveness, a tête-à-tête with our alumnae, recruiter testimonials, parent conversations and finally the batch profile of students. We would like to thank Dr Nilesh Thakre, Faculty and Career Development Chairperson for his guidance throughout this endeavor. We also wish to place on record our heartfelt thanks and appreciation to the alumnae for their constant and unstinting spontaneous support. We've put our heart and soul into bringing out this brochure and hope that you will find it useful for making your recruitment decisions! Welcome!

Note: About 80% of the students from our course were placed. The highest salary offered was Rs. 20,000 per month, while the lowest salary offered was Rs. 10,000 per month.
